Punch reports that the man of God has stated that couples who engage in masturbation, oral sex, doggy style and have sex with the lady on top will go to hell, not heaven!

He made this prophecy in a fifteen-minute video entitled, ‘Warnings to married Christian couples from the Lord Jesus Christ,' where he said people who do anything other than missionary “serve the spirit of lust.”

Oladejo said: “The Lord has made the man the head, not the woman.

“So, those men who enjoy their wives coming on top of them on their matrimonial bed, the Lord says they are not coming to heaven at all!

Moving on to doggy style, the pastor stated that this style is a sin because it turns the woman into a four-footed beast.

He said: “The Lord did not create you to behave like a dog on your matrimonial bed with your wife; or like a goat, or like a cow, or like a chicken.

“Those who do that are not going to make it to heaven.”

“I really didn’t want to share it with people, but the Lord laid it in my heart because He wants to use it to save the souls of His children who are on their way to hell,” he added.

According to him, these people received the worst form of torture.
