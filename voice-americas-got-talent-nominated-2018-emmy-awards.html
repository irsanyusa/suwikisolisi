It’s that time of year again. Emmy nominations were announced today, and The Voice has scored a spot on ten of the shortlists.

The majority of the categories for which the show is in the ring fall under the “Creative Arts” umbrella, which recognize excellence in lighting, production design, makeup, and more. The Voice has also once again been nominated for “Outstanding Reality Competition Program”. The show previously won this category four times, including the last three years.

Fellow NBC reality competition program America’s Got Talent also scored a nomination in the “Outstanding Lighting Design/Lighting Direction For A Variety Series” category for the work of lighting designers Noah Mitz, Ryan Tanker, Michael Berger, Charles Dabezies, and Matthew Benson.

The 70th Emmy Awards telecast will air live coast-to-coast on NBC on Monday, September 17, 2018, at 8 pm EDT / 5 pm PDT. For the complete list of nominations, check out the announcement on the official Emmys website.
