The Masked Singer Season 11 returned on Wednesday night for the Quarterfinals, as the four remaining contestants performed for a chance to move on. At the end of the night, Poodle Moth was unmasked as This Is Us star Chrissy Metz.

Poodle Moth Unmasked on The Masked Singer

During Wednesday’s show, the final four contestants (who made it through the Group A, B, and C Finals) took the stage to perform. That includes Poodle Moth, who received this season’s “Ding Dong, Keep It On” bell from the panel.

Poodle Moth delivered a great performance of “Price Tag” by Jessie J. Unfortunately, it wasn’t enough to send her straight into the Semifinals. She had to compete in a Smackdown against Gumball, who performed “I’m Yours” by Jason Mraz during Wednesday’s show.

Both contestants performed “If I Could Turn Back Time” by Cher in their own styles. Although they were both great, the panel decided to send Gumball to the Semifinals, meaning Poodle Moth had to be unmasked.

Final guesses from the panel about Poodle Moth’s identity included Shania Twain, Faith Hill, and Melissa McCarthy. However, only Jenny McCarthy was correct in guessing This Is Us star Chrissy Metz. A lot of people probably didn’t realize she was such a good singer.

Goldfish, Clock Head Straight to the Semifinals

At the end of Wednesday’s show, Goldfish and Clock earned enough votes from the panel and audience to move straight into the Season 11 Semifinals. Both contestants delivered incredible performances during the show.

First, Clock took the stage to sing “Get On Your Feet” by Gloria Estefan, which made the panel do just that. Later, Goldfish emerged to deliver a timeless rendition of “Unforgettable” by Nat King Cole. We’ll have to wait a little longer to find out which celebrities are under their masks.
