Former Bachelorette star and Dancing With The Stars celebrity contestant, Hannah Brown announced her engagement to boyfriend, Adam Woolard. The couple announced the news on Instagram in a heartfelt post on August 28, 2023.

Hannah Brown Announces Her Engagement

The couple shared photos of the proposal at Bolt Farm Treehouse in Tennessee. In one photo, Brown is seen touching foreheads with Woolard as she subtly shows off her engagement ring. In another photo, the couple is kissing as Woolard gets down on one knee and proposes. The pair appears to be inside the treehouse and are surrounded by candles and red rose petals.

According to sources, Brown and Woolard have been dating since 2021. They met on a dating app and have been inseparable ever since. The couple recently moved to Nashville together and have a dog named Wally.

Brown is no stranger to reality TV. She first appeared on the scene in 2019 when she was a contestant on Colton Underwood’s season of The Bachelor. She was later announced as the lead of The Bachelorette Season 15 and accepted a proposal from Jed Wyatt at the end. However, they called it quits before her season finale aired.

Woolard is a model and entrepreneur. The now-engaged couple are both excited to start their new chapter together as husband and wife. Their love is built on a foundation of unwavering support and genuine affection, qualities that have been essential in shaping their unique bond.

Woolard Proposed to Brown With a Concierge Ring

Woolard discovered the depth of his feelings for Brown within months of dating. He fondly recalled the weekend when Brown met his family, a time he describes as “so effortless,” like she had been a part of the family for years.

For Brown, it’s not a single moment but an accumulation of shared experiences that made her realize the extent of her feelings. The DWTS alum gushed about Woolard’s calming presence and his acceptance of all facets of her personality, even the quirkiest ones.

The engagement ring, a stunning three-stone creation from Ring Concierge, holds a special significance. Woolard’s decision to choose this design underscores their commitment to cherishing each moment while looking ahead to a shared future.
