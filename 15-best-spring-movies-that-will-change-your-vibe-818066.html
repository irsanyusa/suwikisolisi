After Landon’s prank leads to community service, he meets Jamie, a devout girl, during volunteering. Despite their contrasting personalities, Landon is attracted to Jamie’s genuineness, leading to a profound bond. However, Jamie’s revelation of a painful secret challenges their relationship, prompting Landon to reconsider his values and beliefs.

Excited for Easter, Roo plans a festive celebration in the Hundred Acre Wood alongside his friends. However, Rabbit’s decision to forego Easter in favor of spring-cleaning prompts Roo and his pals to remind Rabbit of the significance of traditions and togetherness. With the aid of Tigger, Piglet, Eeyore, and Winnie the Pooh, Roo sets out on a touching quest to restore Easter and rekindle joy in their woodland abode.
